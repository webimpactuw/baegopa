ALL YOU CAN EAT
GUIDELINES

Does every party member get charged AYCE?

Due to the frequent incidents.. tables who order the all you can eat option, every single guest will be charged for the all you can eat price (no exceptions). Those who would not like to eat or are vegetarians will also be charged if they are sitting at the table**

Is free rice available for AYCE customers?

Please let our staff know you would like rice. We have seen an increase amount of food waste for NOT eating your rice. To reduce food waste we will no longer provide rice unless asked.** Thank you for your understanding

When does the AYCE close?

For the best experience (1.5HR), our AYCE menu last call is 1 hour prior to closing. We are more than happy to offer our A la carte, Ready to Eats, and Combos if you come in 1 hour prior to our closing. Thank you!

GENERAL RULES:

Time limit 1.5 hrs
Everyone at the table must order AYCE
Minimum of 2 AYCE orders
Must finish all plates before ordering more
Only 4 items can be ordered at a time
Take out is not allowed
Extra charge of $1.50 for each rice bowl or soup left over
Extra charge of $5 per person for any meat leftover
Food can not be taken to-go
